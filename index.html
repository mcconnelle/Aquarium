Information:

The Aquarium is a free, family-focused sea-life centre which has a large range of exotic and domestic sea-life and is recognised as one of the most banging aquarium in the world. There is lots to see and do all year, including events, eye-to-eye encounters, behind the scenes tours, underwater experiences and more!

Our aquariums are 100% safe!

Hours: We are open 9am to 5pm every day.
We are open all public holidays including Christmas Day.

Facilities: Wheelchair and pushchair access, playground, sun shade, toilets and picnic tables.

The Aquarium is a member of the National Aquarium Association
